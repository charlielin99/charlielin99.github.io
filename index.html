About Me

Hey, I’m Charlie!

I’m a student at Western University looking to pursue a dual degree in Software Engineering and Business Administration. I live at the intersection of innovative technologies and their long-term business applications. I am eclectically curious and am always trying to learn new things. In my free time, I enjoy playing & watching soccer, reading up about the latest developments in space and taking to the air in a Cessna 172, as well as (driving) auto-piloting around the city in my Tesla.
