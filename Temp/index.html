About Me

Hey, I’m Charlie!

I’m a student at Western University looking to pursue a dual degree in Software Engineering and Business Administration. I live at the intersection of innovative technologies and their long-term business applications. I am eclectically curious and am always trying to learn new things. I’m also an avid public speaker and have a strong passion for finance and entrepreneurship.

My most recent positions had me working on fields ranging from Full Stack Development to Deep Learning Research, all the while running a very cool HR-Tech startup. I also lead education sessions on various different languages, frameworks and ML techniques for the largest technology club on campus.

In my free time, I enjoy playing & watching soccer, reading up about the latest developments in space and taking to the air in a Cessna 172, as well as (driving) auto-piloting around the city in my Tesla.
